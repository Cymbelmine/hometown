Top three activities to do in Bochum

Take a tour of a museum

The Bergbaumuseum Bochum is the coal mine museum. You can learn about the Ruhr Valley.

Take a class at the uni

The Ruhr Univeristy is the cultural and educational hub of Bochum.

Gaze at the stars

The Planetarium is a great option for a night of star gazing or light shows and music.
